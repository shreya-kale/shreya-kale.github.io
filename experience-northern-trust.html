Project Overview

Enterprise AI & GenAI-Driven Risk Review and Compliance Intelligence Platform

This project focused on modernizing manual financial risk review and compliance workflows at Northern Trust by building a scalable AI and Generative AI-driven decision-support platform. The existing process relied heavily on manual reviews of transactions, exposure reports, and regulatory documents, which caused delays, high operational cost, and inconsistent risk assessment. The goal was to design predictive risk models, transaction anomaly detection systems, and LLM-powered document intelligence, integrated into a governed MLOps framework, to support analysts with faster, explainable, and auditable insights.
