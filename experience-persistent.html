Project Overview

Enterprise Credit Risk & Real-Time Fraud Detection Platform

This project was focused on building a scalable, data-driven credit risk and fraud detection platform for a financial services client. The client faced challenges with manual underwriting, delayed loan approvals, inaccurate default predictions, and high false fraud alerts. Existing rule-based systems were not adaptive to changing customer behavior and transaction patterns. The objective was to design and deploy machine learning-based credit risk scoring, customer risk segmentation, and real-time fraud detection systems, integrated with cloud infrastructure and production-ready ML services.

Key Achievements

29% Improved Default Prediction
42% Reduced Manual Underwriting
34% Increased Fraud Detection
26% Reduced False Fraud Alerts
18% Faster Decision Turnaround
